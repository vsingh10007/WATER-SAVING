About Water Conservation

Water is life. Yet, we are running out of this precious resource. Learn why conserving water is crucial for our homes, schools, industries, and the planet.

Why is Water Conservation Important?

Freshwater makes up only 2.5% of all water on Earth. Of this, less than 1% is accessible for human use. Rapid population growth, pollution, climate change, and mismanagement have created a global water crisis.

Conserving water ensures that future generations have access to safe and clean water. Every drop saved helps reduce energy use, protect ecosystems, and support sustainable growth.
